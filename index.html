Shri Omkareshwar Temple

The Omkareshwar Temple in Pune is a historic Shiva temple with strong ties to the Peshwa era, particularly during the reign of Chimaji Appa. Built between 1736-1738 by Shivram Bhatt, the spiritual guru of the Peshwas, it's considered one of Pune's oldest and largest temples. The temple is also associated with the samadhi (memorial) of Chimaji Appa.
